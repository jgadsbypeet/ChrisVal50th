How Do I Get There?

Step-by-Step Directions

Address: Curlew Rowing Club, Crane St, London SE10 9DG

  1. Step 1: Travel to Cutty Sark DLR station. This is the easiest public transport option.
  2. Step 2: Exit station and walk toward Greenwich town centre, then follow signs for the riverside. It is approximately a 10-minute walk.
  3. Step 3: If coming via North Greenwich Tube, take a local bus/taxi to Greenwich, then continue on foot to Crane Street.
  4. Step 4: Parking is limited. Try nearby car parks such as Cutty Sark Gardens and allow extra time for walking.

Street parking can be very limited on busy days, so public transport is strongly recommended.
